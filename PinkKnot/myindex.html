About Breast Cancer

Breast cancer is a type of cancer that starts in the breast. Cancer starts when cells begin to grow out of control.Breast cancer cells usually form a tumor that can often felt as a lump. Breast cancer occurs almost entirely in women, but men can get breast cancer too.It is important to understand that most breast lumps are benign and not cancer (malignant).

Breast Cancer Awareness Month

The Breast Cancer Awareness Month, marked in countries across the world every October, helps to increase attention and support for the awareness, early detection and treatment as well as palliative care of this disease.
